Shipping & Delivery

While The Pantry Forge endeavours to provide you with the best delivery experience possible, we are under significant time constraints from external commitments. Below is everything you need to know about our shipping and delivery process. If you have any issues, please contact us.

Order Processing

As a small 3D printing business, we currently don't print most of our models in advance. This means when you place an order, we have to print it before we start preparing it for shipping. Typically, printing takes 2-5 hours depending on the model. Once this is complete, the product undergoes processing, which typically takes 2 non-business days.

Note that high demand or large orders may increase the lead time on your order. Additionally, we take occasional breaks from doing business. If this is the case, or soon to be the case, you will be notified before placing your order. If your order is not en route within a week, please don't hesitate to contact us.

We continue to fulfill orders on weekends and public holidays unless otherwise specified. Indeed, a lot of our processing gets done on these days. Most of our printing is done overnight, and this is why you will often see a delay of up to 12 hours between the order printing and shipping.

International Taxes

Product prices do not include tax. Tax is calculated at checkout. The customer is responsible for all import duties and GST. Note that prices include GST for Australia only.

As our products are almost completely plastic, they should not be significantly delayed by customs, however there is always a possibility. If this occurs, it should appear on your tracking information.

Delivery

Deliveries are not managed by The Pantry Forge. For information on this matter, please see the AusPost Parcel Delivery page.

If you accidentally entered the wrong delivery address, please contact us as soon as possible. If your parcel hasn't been sent yet, we'll change the delivery address for you. Otherwise, you'll have to contact Australia Post to redirect your parcel. Incorrect address is entirely your responsibility.

If your order fails to deliver and is returned to us, we'll attempt to contact you and arrange a solution. If we can't contact you, we'll generally keep the order and give you a full refund.

Lost, Delayed or Damaged Shipments

All Pantry Forge orders ship with tracking included. If your item is lost or delayed during shipment, please contact us, and we'll follow up with Australia Post on your order. If your order arrives damaged, contact us and we'll resolve the matter, normally with a full refund or replacement package.

Partial Shipments

Most orders ship in one package. If not, we'll let you know before sending it, and we'll only charge the originally listed shipping cost. You'll be provided with multiple tracking numbers for your parcels.

Sustainability

As part of our effort to minimise the environmental impact of our business, all orders ship with recyclable packaging. Furthermore, we endeavour to use the lease amount of packaging possible while still keeping your order safe, reducing the amount of waste going into turtles' bellies.

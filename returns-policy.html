Returns, Refunds & Exchanges Policy

Introduction

This policy covers returns, refunds and exchanges for all purchases made from The Pantry Forge, and is applicable to all products we sell.

All goods and services we provide are subject to Australian Consumer Law (ACL) guarantees. This Returns, Refunds & Exchanges Policy (Policy) operates in addition to your rights under the ACL. Nothing in this Policy is intended to exclude, restrict, or modify any statutory rights or remedies available to you under ACL.

This Policy is governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Australia and New South Wales.

Definitions

  • Return: The process of returning a product to us for assessment where a customer believes the product does not comply with the Australian Consumer Law, or where a return is permitted under this Policy.
  • Refund: A repayment of the amount paid for a product, provided where required under the Australian Consumer Law (including for a major failure), or otherwise in accordance with this Policy.
  • Exchange: The replacement of a product with the same or a comparable product, where permitted under this Policy or as a remedy available under the Australian Consumer Law, subject to availability.
  • Faulty or Defective: A product that does not meet the consumer guarantees under the Australian Consumer Law, including but not limited to where it is unsafe, not of acceptable quality, not fit for purpose, or does not match its description, and where the issue is not caused by misuse, accidental damage, or normal wear and tear.
  • Change of Mind: A return request made where a product is not faulty and complies with the Australian Consumer Law, but the customer decides they no longer want the product, have ordered incorrectly, or no longer require it.
  • Unused, Original Condition: A product that has not been used beyond what is reasonably necessary to assess it, and is returned in a condition consistent with reasonable consumer inspection, including with original packaging, labels, and seals where practicable.
  • Proof of Purchase: Reasonable evidence that a product was purchased from us, as recognised under the Australian Consumer Law, including a receipt, invoice, order confirmation, or transaction record.
  • Store Credit: A credit issued for future purchases, offered at our discretion where permitted by law, and which does not replace or limit any refund entitlement required under the Australian Consumer Law.
  • Services: Any services supplied by us within the meaning of the Australian Consumer Law, including preparation, customisation, or other non-physical services, where applicable.
  • Custom, Made-to-Order, or Personalised Items: Products made to a customer's specific requirements that are not ordinarily offered for general sale, noting that consumer guarantees under the Australian Consumer Law still apply to such products.

Your Rights

Under Australian law, consumers have a set of rights when buying products that cannot be revoked. For information on your rights, see the ACCC website.

By law, you are entitled to these rights. We cannot overrule these rights through policy. This Policy operates in conjunction with ACL. Note that ACL does not require businesses to provide Change of Mind Returns or Exchanges.

Our Rights

We maintain the right to refuse service of suspected excessive or fraudulent Returns. Should you abuse this Policy, your right to return products will be limited to the extent permitted by ACL. Should you continue abuse, we will take further action to reduce your ability to engage with our store.

We reserve the right to update, modify, or replace this Policy at any time without prior notice. Continued use of our Services after any changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy. Changes are effective from the date of publication.

Change of Mind Returns

To be eligible for a Change of Mind Return, the product must meet ALL of the following requirements.

  • Either:
    1. The product was purchased within the last fifteen (15) days OR
    2. The product was delivered within the last seven (7) days and a dated proof of delivery is provided.
  • A Proof of Purchase is provided.
  • All parts of the original product are in Unused, Original Condition.
  • The product is not Made-to-Order.
  • The price per unit of the product at the time of purchase was under one hundred (100) Australian Dollars.
  • The total price of the product/s to be Returned was under five hundred (500) Australian Dollars at the time of purchase.
  • The product was not part of a clearance sale at the time of purchase.
  • The product is not perishable.
  • The product is not a gift voucher.
  • The product is a physical item.
  • The product is not intended for personal use and, due to health or safety considerations, is not suitable for return if opened, used, or otherwise compromised.
  • The product is not damaged due to misuse.

If your product is eligible for a Change of Mind Return, and you would like to return it, contact us. We will provide instructions on how to return the product. Once we receive the product, we will inspect it and determine if it is eligible. If it is, we will provide you with a Refund, Exchange or Store Credit.

Note that shipping costs are not refundable for Change of Mind Returns. Additionally, if a product is on sale at the time of purchase, only the amount that it was purchased for will be eligible for Refund or Store Credit.

Faulty, Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you believe an item to be faulty, damaged or incorrect, contact us with details and evidence. To be eligible for Return, the item must either:

  1. Not meet Consumer Rights as outlined under ACL, OR
  2. Meet ALL of the following criteria:
    • The item is faulty, damaged or incorrect.
    • Photographic evidence is provided of the fault, damage or error.
    • The product is a physical item.
    • The product is not damaged due to misuse.
    • Either:
      1. The product was purchased within the last sixty (60) days OR
      2. The product was delivered within the last fifty (50) days and a dated proof of delivery is provided.

When you report an item to us as faulty, damaged or incorrect, we will assess the situation to determine if it is eligible for Return. If it is eligible, we will contact you and provide you with a Refund, Repair, Exchange or Store Credit. Should you choose a Repair, we will provide it for free, and will cover the costs of shipping the item back for repair. Should you choose an Exchange, we will provide free shipping for the replacement item. Should you choose a Refund, Exchange or Store Credit, you will be able to keep the item, dispose of it, or return it to us at your expense.

Repairs and Exchanges may not be available due to low supply. If this is the case, we will contact you and provide you with other options.

Exchanges

If you enact a Change of Mind Return or the product is faulty, damaged or incorrect, and you choose to Exchange the product, you can choose any other item from our online store providing it meets the following criteria:

  • The new item is available for purchase at the time of the Exchange.
  • The new item is the same product as the bought item. Note that it can be a different variant, e.g. size, colour.
  • The price of the new item at the time of exchange is equal to the price of the bought item at the time of purchase. (If you want another product that is a different price, request a Refund or Store Credit.)

Refunds

If you are eligible for and request a Refund, you will be able to choose from the following methods:

  • Original Payment Method
  • Store Credit
  • Bank Transfer (Australia only)
  • PayPal

We are able to provide a partial refund. Timeframes on refunds will depend on the payment method, so we will inform you of this at the time of Refund.

Store Credit

Store Credit is non-transferrable and non-refundable. It is issued in the form of an electronic gift card, to be used in our online store. It may include an expiry period.

The Return Process

Start by contacting us with your personal details, your order details, and the reason you would like to return the product. We will contact you with information regarding eligibility, return address and payments.

If you live outside of Australia, there may be difficulty in arranging a Return, Repair or Exchange. Whilst we strive to deliver the best experience possible, we may not be able to issue you a full Refund.

When you return an item, please make sure to package it securely. If the item is damaged in transit, you may not be eligible for reimbursements. Ideally, use the original packaging.

If we determine your item is not eiligible for return, we will return it to you at your cost, or provide you with a partial Refund.
